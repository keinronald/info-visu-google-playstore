Google Play Store Breakdown

At this page, we are aiming to understand the trend of current Google Playstore market.

Which categories are popular/crowded?

As we can see in the visualisation Family is by far (873 Apps) the category with the most apps (most crowded) on the Google Play Store. But if we switch the y-axis to "Number of Installs" we can see that Game is the most popular Category followed by Communication. So they have less competition but more installs.

Reviews and their Impact

It also is clear that the amount of reviews has a positive impact on the rating. We can also see that the three apps with the most reviews are all social media Apps and the most rated App in the Paid section is Minecraft. When we observe the different datatypes (all/free/paid Apps) we can clearly see that there isn't a lot of difference between all Apps and free Apps, so we can think that most apps are free. Let's check this theory in the next visualization.

Note: Difference between all and free Apps is minimal.

Are really most Apps free?

Yes - When we observe the pair to free Apps ration in the top 5 Categories we can clearly see that our theory is right and most of the Apps in the Google Play Store are Free. Most categories only have a maximum of about 10% Paid Apps.

Note: Visualization is in Numbers of Apps.

Which Content Ratings are popular/crowded?

We can clearly see that Everyone is by far the most popular and most crowded Content Rating group.

Conclusio

So if we want to develop our own app we learned that:

  • Gaming and Communication are the most downloaded Categories,
  • there are far more Free Apps in the Google Play Store,
  • the Content Rating Everyone is unsurprisingly the most popluar and most crowded one
  • and if we manage to get a huge amount of reviews it is likely that the average rating is better (because we have a big user base).
